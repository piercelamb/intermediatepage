The SnappyData Team

We have spent the past decade building in-memory data products together. Our goal is to take the lessons we’ve learned in distributed data management and apply them to the rapidly expanding world of distributed data processing. We look for people that are motivated by challenges and want to work on the cutting edge of the big data industry.

Meet our founders

The "three amigos" have been building data products together for over a decade.

  • Richard Lamb, CEO

    Richard Lamb is a founder and the CEO of SnappyData. Previously, Richard was the Vice President of Real Time Solutions at Pivotal and was a worldwide evangelist for their in-memory database GemFire. Richard was one of the original leaders of the GemFire product at GemStone Inc where GemFire was authored and eventually sold to VMWare.

  • Jags Ramnarayan, CTO

  • Sudhir Menon, COO

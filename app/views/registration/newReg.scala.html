Precis

By executing queries over multi-dimensional samples of very large datasets, Precis enables you to control the latency of your SQL queries with very high bounded accuracy

  • Trade off accuracy of SQL queries for response time
  • Execute queries on historical or streaming data
  • Linearly scalable, highly available, massively parallel design

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Precis works by automatically maintaining multi-dimensional samples of original data and selecting the right sample based on latency or error specified
